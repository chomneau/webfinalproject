Get ready to kickstart your student life! At AUPP we understand that your college experience is not just about academics but also about personal growth, community engagement, and well-rounded development. Our Student Affairs team is committed to enhancing your journey by providing a range of services and opportunities that extend beyond the classroom. From counseling and wellness programs to student clubs, leadership development initiatives, and career growth, we aim to create an inclusive and supportive environment where you can thrive. Explore the various resources available, connect with fellow students, and take advantage of the diverse array of activities designed to enrich your college experience. At AUPP, Student Affairs is not just a department—it’s a vibrant community dedicated to helping you make the most of your college journey and prepare you for success both in and beyond academia.
