Our campus sits on 7 hectares of land with one of the most modern facilities. The campus features a large parking area, its very own sports club, and most importantly the American-inspired main building. The campus offers students the same facilities that you may find in American universities such as American-style lecture halls and classrooms, a spacious library and learning center, a Center for student involvement, computer lab, and more. AUPP is ready to provide students with the most enjoyable studying experience with the sensation of studying abroad while staying locally.

In-person Campus Tour

Our in-person campus tour includes an overview of our campus facilities along with student services, student activities, and more. If you would like to visit our campus physically, please contact us at either (+855) 23 990 023 or info@aupp.edu.kh.
